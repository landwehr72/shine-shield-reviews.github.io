About Salt Life Detail

Honest detailing product reviews from Florida's coast

Our Story

Salt Life Detail was born from a passion for keeping boats and cars looking their best in Florida's challenging coastal environment. Between the salt air, intense UV rays, and year-round outdoor lifestyle, we needed products that actually work – not just marketing hype.

Our Promise

Every product we review has been personally purchased and tested. No sponsored content, no affiliate bias – just honest reviews from someone who actually uses this stuff on boats and cars.

Our Testing Process

  • Real-world conditions: We test in actual saltwater and freshwater environments
  • Long-term testing: Products are evaluated over months, not days
  • Multiple vehicles: Testing on different paint types and surfaces
  • Honest documentation: Photos and detailed notes throughout the process
